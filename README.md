# Avatar: The Arena

A multi-level arena game developed in C++ using the SFML library. This project is inspired by the animated series Avatar: The Last Airbender.

## Gameplay Demonstration
A video showcasing the gameplay can be found here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h5CuwwU9Zug

## Game Objective
The goal is to progress through all stages by capturing altars and defeating enemies. The journey leads to a final confrontation against the Fire Lord. The game is successfully completed once the final boss is defeated.

## Stages and Progression
The game consists of five distinct stages:
* Air
* Water
* Earth
* Fire
* Final Boss Battle

On each stage, the player must eliminate a specific number of enemies and capture an altar. Progressing through these stages unlocks elemental abilities. To acquire a new skill, the player must reach the required kill count and interact with a teacher NPC to collect the ability.

## Elemental Abilities
Each element grants three unique abilities mapped to specific controls:

| Control | Air | Water | Earth | Fire |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **LMB** | Windgust | Ice-shard shot | Flying rock | Fireball |
| **Shift** | Speed boost | Heal | Temporary armor | Additional damage |
| **Space** | Avoid projectiles | Ice avalanche | Rock boulder | Ring of fire |

## Bestiary
Each stage features unique enemies with dedicated textures and animations:
* **Air:** Practice doll
* **Water:** Water slime
* **Earth:** Earth golem
* **Fire:** Fire snake
* **Universal:** Fire Nation soldiers appear across all levels.

## The Final Boss
The Fire Lord serves as the ultimate challenge, featuring enhanced health, movement speed, and damage compared to standard enemies.
